#classy

Your Virtual Closet

#classy is a web app that allows users to create and manage their virtual closet. Users can add new collections/items to their closet. #classy was built on on HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery on the frontend and Ruby on Rails, PSQL for backend/database.

Survey Me

Create a survey question and collect the answers.

Survey Me is a group project in which I was the lead front-end developer. Survey me lets user create a survey question then generate a random URL which is then used to send out to survey takers. Once survey takers submit the survey answers, the answers will be collected and saved to database and displayed on user's dashboard view. We used Javascript, jQuery on the front-end and Express, MongoDB for backend/database, git/github to manage our workflow.

Wanderlust

Create a trip that becomes your lifetime experience.

Wanderlust is a web app that allows user to plan their upcoming trips. Integrated with Google Maps API, users could search for their new destination on the map and add/edit trip details. Wanderlust was built on HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery on the frontend and Ruby on Rails, PSQL for backend/database.
